Tiramisu

Description

A mascarpone cake with coffee-dipped lady finger biscuits

Ingredients

  • Lady Fingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Coco Powder
  1. Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together until stiff peaks.
  2. Add the espresso and liqueur (if using) to a shallow bowl and dip the lady fingers on both sides (don't let them soak, just a quick dip!)
  3. Smooth a layer of the mascarpone/whipped cream mixture on top of the lady fingers.
  4. Add another layer of lady fingers (dipped in coffee and liqueur) and another layer of cheese mixture. Dust with cocoa powder.
