Chocolate chips cookies

Description

Basic Chocolate Chip cookies with a soft center

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Egg yolk
  • Vanilla
  • Brown Sugar
  • White Sugar
  • Chocolate Chips
  • CornStarch
  • Salt
  • Baking Soda
  • Melted Butter
  1. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Cream/Mix the sugars and butter together in a different bowl
  3. Add eggs and vanilla to the sugar and butter mix
  4. Combine the the dry ingredients with the sugar/butter mixture
  5. Add the chocolate chips to the dough
  6. Portion out the dough mixture into even size balls
  7. Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes
